Hamburger!

Recipe description:

This recipe is the best for any event! It is easy to make, and delicious to eat.

The recipe uses all easily obtainable ingredients, ensuring that you can make it anywhere!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Dice onions and garlic
  2. Shred cheese
  3. Combine all ingredients into bowl, mix finely
  4. Make into balls, then add into pan, and smash to form patties
  5. Flip every so often, and enjoy!
