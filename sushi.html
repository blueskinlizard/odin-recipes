Sushi Roll!

Recipe description:

While sushi is never thought of in a "homemade" sense, our recipe solves this issue and gives you the ability to make a delicious salmon role. .

We thought of price specifically in this recipe, so you can make a serving for under $10.00.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Sticky/sushi rice, or check out this recipe to make your own
  • Nori seaweed
  • Rice vinegar
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber

Instructions:

  1. Mix rice vinegar along with extra salt and sugar to fully prepare our sticky rice.
  2. Lay nori sheets flatly, and heat in oven for 1-2 minutes
  3. Lay rice onto nori sheet, and add salmon, avocado, and cucumber(Lay it preferrably onto a bamboo sheet)
  4. After complete rolls have been formed, slice it to make little portions
  5. Enjoy!
